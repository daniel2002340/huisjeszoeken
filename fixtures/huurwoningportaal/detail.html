2 kamer appartement in Delft

Description. To make this process as fair as possible, you can only apply digitally*. Fantastic unfurnished 1-bedroom apartment on the 6th floor, with a separate storage room in the basement, a bike storage area and lift access!. The location is unbeatable. In less than a minute’s walk, you reach the covered shopping center, where you can do your daily grocery shopping at the supermarkets and fresh food stores, grab a quick lunch or coffee, or visit practical shops such as the pharmacy and florist. Living above such a complete shopping center makes daily life exceptionally convenient.. Just around the corner and within about ten minutes you’ll find yourself in Delft’s historic city center. Explore charming canals, historic buildings, and bustling squares. The city offers a wide range of shops, boutiques, and specialty stores, as well as numerous restaurants and cafes. For breakfast or lunch, Stads-Koffyhuis is a popular choice, while LEF Restaurant & Bar Delft, Spijshuis de Dis, and Huszár are all within easy reach for a cozy dinner.. Accessibility is excellent. Delft Station is only a few minutes’ walk or bike ride away, and from there you can reach Rotterdam or The Hague in about 15 minutes by train. By car, the A13 and A4 motorways are quickly accessible, making travel throughout the region easy.. Layout. The building’s ground floor features a neat, secured entrance with a modern intercom system. Here you will find the mailboxes, a convenient parcel locker for deliveries when you’re not home, a bike storage area, and a designated space for waste disposal. The building has five elevators, including two extra-large ones ideal for moving or transporting bikes. Access via the stairs is also available.. Inside the apartment, the spacious living room is filled with natural light from the large windows. The open kitchen is fully equipped with a cooktop, combi-oven/microwave, dishwasher, refrigerator, and freezer, providing everything you need for comfortable cooking.. The bathroom is fully equipped with a nice walk-in shower, a large sink, and a mirror. Additionally, it offers ample storage space. The bathroom will be renovated, so the start date might change depending on the progress of the renovation work.. The bedroom is generously sized, accommodating a double bed and a large wardrobe. Both the living room and bedroom offer access to the long balcony that spans the entire length of the apartment – the perfect spot to enjoy fresh air and sunshine.. Additional Information:. - Start date: early August. The bathroom is being completely renovated, so the exact start date is not yet known;. - Rent excludes heating, water, electricity, internet/TV, and municipal taxes.. - Rent increase scheduled from 01-07-2027.. - Minimum rental period: 12 months.. - Energy label: A.. - Advance payment for heating costs: €100 per month.. - Advance payment for service costs: €95 per month.. - (Bicycle) storage available.. - Parking space available for rent from €121 per month (mandatory if you own a car).. Income requirement: minimum gross monthly income of 3.5x the base rent
